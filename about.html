About Us

Welcome to our company, where we specialize in bringing the evergreen tunes to life. Our passion for music and history has driven us to collect, analyze and restore ancient record vinyl players, allowing us to share the rich sounds of vinyl records with a new generation of music lovers. Our journey began with me restoring my father's old record player which had been un-functional for a very long time. During this activity I came across the irreplaceable warmth and charm of vinyl. This is the reason that our company is dedicated to bringing the vintage charm of record players to your home, while also providing high-quality modern components for an exceptional listening experience. Our products are carefully curated and tested to ensure that you get the best possible sound quality from your vinyl collection. We understand the significance of record players as a cultural icon, and we strive to provide products that honor that legacy. We offer a wide variety of record players, from vintage-inspired designs to sleek, modern options, so that you can find the perfect fit for your taste and lifestyle. Our commitment to customer satisfaction is unwavering, and we take pride in providing outstanding customer service. Whether you are a seasoned vinyl collector or just starting your collection, we are here to help you choose the perfect record player for your needs.

Our Mission

At our core, we are passionate about music and believe that vinyl is a medium that deserves to be celebrated. Our mission is to provide our customers with high-quality vinyl record players that deliver a rich and authentic sound experience. We believe that music is not just a form of entertainment, but a way of life that should be cherished and enjoyed to the fullest. We are committed to sustainability and reducing our environmental impact. Thus, we carefully source our materials, and all of our products are made with eco-friendly materials and processes. Our customers are our top priority, and we strive to provide exceptional customer service. We understand that buying a record player is an investment, and we want our customers to feel confident in their purchase. Our knowledgeable and friendly customer service team is always available to answer questions and provide support. Our mission is to be the premier vinyl record player company, providing our customers with the highest quality products and unparalleled customer service. Thank you for considering our company, and we look forward to sharing our passion for music with you.
